About LocalMart

LocalMart is a hyperlocal electronics marketplace built to make buying and selling second-hand electronics in your community easy, safe, and transparent.

🛒 What We Do

We connect local buyers and sellers of electronics — phones, laptops, accessories, and more. Sellers list products in minutes; buyers browse verified listings nearby and make secure purchases.

⚙️ How It Works

Sellers sign up, create a store, and list products with photos, descriptions, pricing, and condition. Buyers filter by category or price, view product details, and add items to their cart.

🔒 Trust & Safety

Every seller is verified via phone and email. Buyer reviews are visible on each listing. All data is securely stored through Supabase (PostgreSQL).

🛠️ Tech Stack

Pure HTML, CSS & JavaScript frontend · Node.js / Express backend on Render · Supabase for database and auth.
